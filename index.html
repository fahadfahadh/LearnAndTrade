Trading Philosophy

Trade Smarter with Defined Risk

Our approach centers on strategies where the maximum loss is known before entering the trade. No surprises, no panic — just disciplined execution with index options.

Bull Call Spread

A defined-risk bullish strategy using two call options at different strikes. Benefit from upside moves while capping maximum loss to the premium paid.

Limited Loss

Bear Put Spread

A defined-risk bearish strategy with two put options. Profit from downward moves in the index with a pre-defined maximum risk amount.

Limited Loss

Iron Condor

Collect premium when the market stays range-bound. Combines a bull put spread and bear call spread for income in sideways markets.

Range Bound

Straddle / Strangle

Profit from volatility regardless of direction. Ideal for event-driven moves like budget day, RBI policy, or earnings announcements.

High Volatility
